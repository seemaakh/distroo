DISTRO App

The DISTRO app is available on the Google Play Store and Apple App Store. Search for "DISTRO Nepal" or use the download links on our homepage.
Download the app, tap "Register My Store", and enter your store name, address, and phone number. You'll receive an OTP to verify. Once verified, your account is active and you can start ordering immediately.
To change your phone number, go to Settings → Account → Phone Number in the app, or contact our customer support directly. Identity verification will be required.
Yes. You can place orders via WhatsApp by messaging our business number. You can also request an in-person visit from your DISTRO field agent who will take your order directly.

Order-related information

Open the app, browse the catalogue by category or search for a product, add items to your cart with the desired quantities, and tap "Place Order". You'll receive a confirmation and estimated delivery time immediately.
The minimum order value is ₹500. There is no maximum limit. For orders above ₹10,000, you may qualify for additional bulk discounts — contact your field agent for details.
Orders placed before 2 PM are delivered the same day in Kathmandu Valley. Orders placed after 2 PM are delivered the following morning. For other districts, delivery is within 24–48 hours depending on your location.
You can cancel an order within 30 minutes of placing it through the app. After that, the order is processed for dispatch and cannot be cancelled.
We accept cash on delivery, digital wallets (eSewa, Khalti, IME Pay), bank transfer, and DISTRO Credit (buy now, pay in 15 days). Choose your preferred method at checkout.
Yes. Contact customer support via the app or WhatsApp at least 3 hours before the scheduled delivery window and we will reschedule to your preferred time slot.

Problems with my order

First check your order status in the app. If it shows "Delivered" but you haven't received it, contact support immediately with your order number. We'll investigate and, if confirmed, redeliver or issue a full refund within 24 hours.
Take a photo of the damage and report it within 24 hours via the app (Order History → Report Issue) or WhatsApp. We'll arrange a replacement delivery or credit to your account.
Report the wrong item via the app within 24 hours. Keep the incorrectly delivered item — our delivery team will collect it when they bring your correct product, at no extra charge.
Returns are accepted within 48 hours for damaged, expired, or incorrectly delivered items. Perishable goods cannot be returned. Contact support to initiate a return — we'll schedule a pickup and process your refund or credit within 3 working days.
💳

DISTRO Credit

Your credit limit is based on your order history and business profile. New customers start with ₹5,000. As you build a track record, your limit increases automatically — up to ₹50,000 for established customers.
Your credit limit renews immediately upon payment. If you pay your outstanding balance today, you can use the full credit limit on your next order.
You have 15 days from the delivery date to pay. There is zero interest during this period. Late payments incur a 2% fee per week on the outstanding amount.
You need to have placed at least 3 orders with DISTRO and maintained a good payment history. Your field agent can check if you're pre-approved and help activate your credit line.
Credit repayment is accepted via cash, eSewa, Khalti, IME Pay, and direct bank transfer. Cash payments can be made to your field agent during their weekly visit.
💰

Top-ups and bill payments

You can pay electricity (NEA), water (KUKL/municipality), internet, and cable TV bills directly from the DISTRO app. Just link your consumer number and pay in seconds.
Mobile top-ups and bill payments are free of charge for DISTRO store partners. There are no hidden fees or service charges.
You can top up Ncell, Nepal Telecom (NTC), and Smart Cell directly from the app. Recharges are instant and available 24/7.
🤝

Field Agents

A DISTRO field agent is a dedicated business ally assigned to your store. They visit you weekly, help you place orders, offer product recommendations, assist with DISTRO Credit, and resolve any issues you have.
A field agent is automatically assigned when you register your store. You can also request to meet your agent by clicking "Schedule Agent Visit" on the app or our website.
Yes. Your field agent can collect cash payments for orders and DISTRO Credit repayments during their regular weekly visit. Always request a receipt.

Billing

All invoices are available in the app under Account → Invoices. You can download them as PDFs at any time. Invoices are also sent to your registered email after each delivery.
Yes. Contact your field agent or our support team to request branded VAT invoices for your business records. These can be issued within 3 working days of your order delivery.
Invoices are automatically generated upon delivery confirmation. You'll receive an in-app notification and email within 15 minutes of your order being marked as delivered.
Your billing number is displayed in the invoice PDF and in the Order History screen of the app. For any billing disputes, please contact support with your billing number and order date.
